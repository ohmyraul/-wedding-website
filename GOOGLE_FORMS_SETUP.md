# Google Forms Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Google Form

1. Go to [forms.google.com](https://forms.google.com)
2. Click **"Blank"** to create a new form
3. Name it: "Shubs & Alysha Wedding RSVP"

## Step 2: Add Form Fields

Add these questions (matching your current form):

1. **Full Name(s)** - Short answer (required)
2. **Email** - Short answer (required)
3. **Phone** - Short answer (required)
4. **Number of Guests** - Multiple choice:
   - 1 Guest
   - 2 Guests
   - 3 Guests
   - 4 Guests
5. **Will you be attending?** - Multiple choice:
   - Count Me In
   - Cannot Attend
6. **Dietary Restrictions** - Short answer (optional)
7. **Song Request** - Short answer (optional)

## Step 3: Get Your Form Link

1. Click the **"Send"** button (top right)
2. Click the **link icon** (ðŸ”—)
3. Check **"Shorten URL"** (optional, makes link shorter)
4. **Copy the link** - it will look like:
   `https://forms.gle/xxxxxxxxxx`

## Step 5: View Responses

All RSVPs will automatically save to a Google Sheet:
1. In your Google Form, click **"Responses"** tab
2. Click the green **Google Sheets icon** to create a spreadsheet
3. All submissions will appear there automatically!
